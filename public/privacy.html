Privacy Policy

LoKey Typer — Last updated: February 7, 2026

Summary

LoKey Typer does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All user data stays on your device.

What data is stored

The following data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and never leaves your device:

  • User preferences (sound settings, mode selection, accessibility options)
  • Typing run history (WPM, accuracy, timestamps)
  • Personal best records
  • Daily set progress
  • Ambient track play history

What data is NOT collected

  • No personal information (name, email, location)
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No cookies (other than browser-standard service worker cache)
  • No third-party tracking scripts
  • No advertising
  • No cloud sync or remote storage

Accounts

LoKey Typer does not require or support user accounts. There is no login, registration, or authentication.

Network requests

The app makes network requests only to load its own assets (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, audio files) from the hosting server. No data is sent to any server. After the first load, the app works fully offline via a service worker.

Third-party services

LoKey Typer does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Children

LoKey Typer does not knowingly collect data from children. Since no data is collected from any user, no special provisions are needed.
