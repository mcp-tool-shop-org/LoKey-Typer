Welcome to the LoKey Typer handbook. This guide covers installation, practice modes, sound design, personalization, and accessibility.

## What is LoKey Typer?

LoKey Typer is a typing practice app built for adults who want quiet, focused sessions without gamification, leaderboards, or distractions. All data stays on your device. No accounts. No cloud. No tracking.
